Birge

This project is focused on creating a mobile application that promotes mental health and well-being. The interface includes various features such as meditation exercises, a journaling tool, and an appointment booking system.

Problem

Depression and anxiety increased by 25% in the first year of the pandemic, bringing the total number of people living with mental disorders to almost 1 billion, according to the World Mental Health's Report released earlier in 2023.

Goal

The goal is to develop a comprehensive mobile application focused on mental health and wellness for girls and women.

Main Features

  • User-friendly navigation with a focus on mental health resources.
  • Calming interface designed to reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Integrated tools for meditation, journaling, and appointment booking.

Additional Features

  • Customizable reminders for daily wellness activities.
  • Community space to connect with like-minded individuals.
  • Personalized recommendations based on user preferences.

Meditation Library

Offers a variety of guided meditations and calming audio tracks to help users relax and unwind.

Interactive Calendar

Users can easily schedule personal wellness sessions or view their upcoming appointments.

Journaling Tool

A user-friendly interface for tracking daily moods, thoughts, and reflections.
